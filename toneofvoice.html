my voice, tone and style. 

hello, i'm honza. let's pick apart my voice, tone and style.                        

voice

honest. sometimes to a fault, i can be honest about stuff that hurts the other person, but often times it helps people trust my opinion when they really need it.  

informative. i will tell you all you need to know without pleasantries, in as few words as possible. 

hesitant. sometimes my voice can make it seem that i'm not sure what's going on, or that i'm not sure about myself, it's not really a rule as much as a side effect of my anxiety.

tone

distant. you might think i don't like you at first, it can be a curse, but it also allows me to watch social interactions from afar before i decide how to approach it, this can be very helpful.

understanding. even if i'm getting annoyed or tired i will try to offer my help, and try to understand what something feels like from your point of view.

direct. i do everything last minute, so if we are discussing business, i might be in a rush and need to get stuff done, which can come off as direct or straight to the point.

style

self-humouring. i like to entertain myself when talking to people, a friendship usually blossoms when people start to catch on.

casual. even in settings where it's not ideal.

lowercase. purely stylistic... it can make things feel more casual too.
